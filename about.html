ABOUT ME

>

My name is Jess! I'm 20 and currently a design/business student in NY. I dabble in a lot of different art styles and mediums, but my favorite thing to do is create characters and flesh them out through art, writing, etc etc. A lot of my art revolves around music as well, as it is one of my biggest inspirations. I like going to concerts, meeting bands I love, and giving them art related to their music.

I started posting my art onlne when I was 14, and through that I've connected and collaberated wtih some amazing other artists, and been showcased & reposted by people I look up to! Now I'm able to sell work and get it out into the world.

In high school, I realized being creative full-time was actually feasible. This is my attempt to make that happen. :)
